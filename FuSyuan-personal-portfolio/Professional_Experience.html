Empowering Data Discovery and Machine Learning: My Journey at MoBagel

As a passionate advocate of data-driven decision-making and machine learning, my career at MoBagel has been an exciting journey of collaboration, knowledge sharing, and empowering clients to harness the full potential of our cutting-edge AutoMachine Learning tools -- Decanter AI. Throughout my time at MoBagel, I have had the privilege of working closely with data scientists from a Fortune 500 company, delivering speeches and workshops, collaborating with IT teams, and contributing to our product's user manual. Here's a glimpse into the highlights of my experience:

1. Empowering Data Scientists for Data Discovery and Analysis

A significant part of my role at MoBagel involved engaging with data scientists from BD -- a Fortune 500 company to conduct customer sessions for data discovery and analysis. These sessions were crucial in helping our clients unleash the potential of their data and gain meaningful insights through machine learning in the supply-chain industry.
I took pride in providing instant assistance to our clients when they encountered challenges while using MoBagel's Machine Learning tools. Whether it was navigating the Decanter AI functionality or addressing usability questions, my goal was to ensure that our clients could leverage the full power of our platform effectively. One of the key aspects of my role was consulting with clients on how to group prediction results by features, labels, and other relevant factors. Additionally, I assisted them in data preprocessing, offering guidance on data mapping with popular tools like Excel, R, or Python.

2. Knowledge Empowerment through Speeches and Workshops

In my time at MoBagel, I had the opportunity to share my expertise and knowledge of data science and machine learning by delivering three impactful speeches and holding interactive workshops. These events were designed to empower our clients and equip them with the skills they needed to navigate the world of data science confidently.

During these engagements, I covered various topics, from the fundamentals of data science to the latest trends and best practices in machine learning. Through hands-on workshops, participants had the chance to put theory into practice, gaining valuable experience in utilizing MoBagel's tools effectively. Witnessing the newfound confidence and enthusiasm among the attendees was truly rewarding.

3. Enhancing User Experience through User Manual Content Creation

One of my most fulfilling contributions during my time at MoBagel was creating content for the product's user manual. I focused on enhancing the user experience for our clients, making it easier for them to utilize MoBagel's powerful Machine Learning tools efficiently. It's now on the product landing page on MoBagel's Chinese official website.
The user manual contained comprehensive guides on machine learning and artificial intelligence, tailored specifically to our product's functionalities. By providing clear, step-by-step instructions and practical examples, I aimed to empower our clients to navigate the platform seamlessly, extract valuable insights from their data, and make data-driven decisions confidently.

4. Tailored Solutions for Automated Model Training

One of the key highlights of my work at MoBagel was assisting clients who required automated model training and predictive solutions. Through our powerful product API code in Python, we offered them the ability to leverage the full potential of machine learning in an automated and efficient manner.
By carefully understanding each client's unique requirements and business goals, we customized the product API code to align perfectly with their needs. This tailor-made approach ensured that our clients could achieve accurate predictions and gain valuable insights from their data, leading to data-driven decision-making and enhanced business outcomes.

5. Promote the company's products and corporate culture through external activities.

Conclusion

My journey at MoBagel has been filled with valuable experiences, empowering collaborations, and a deep passion for data science and machine learning. Every aspect of my work has contributed to the growth and success of our clients.

As I continue to learn and evolve in the dynamic field of data science, I am grateful for the opportunities that MoBagel has provided me. My favorite part of this job is that I got a chance to work with really young and talented coworkers. The spirits that are shared by the people here are really impressive and unforgettable!

The knowledge gained, the connections forged, and the impact made on our clients' data-driven journey will forever be cherished, and I look forward to further exploring the possibilities of data science and machine learning in shaping a better future.

Empowering Innovation as a Big Data Engineer Intern at H2U: A Journey of Data Excellence

During my time as a Big Data Engineer Intern at H2U, I had the incredible opportunity to contribute significantly to the company's data-driven initiatives, leaving a lasting impact on its data infrastructure and analytics capabilities. Through my dedication, passion, and technical expertise, I spearheaded several transformative projects that harnessed the power of big data and advanced analytics.

1. Propelling Graph Analysis for Enhanced Marketing Strategies

As a forward-thinking intern at H2U, I saw the potential of graph analysis in revolutionizing marketing strategies and customer engagement. I proposed this innovative idea to H2U's senior management, showcasing the immense value of uncovering hidden connections and relationships within the company's data ecosystem.
My presentation to the leadership team highlighted how graph analysis could optimize targeted marketing efforts, leading to increased customer satisfaction and business growth. I was thrilled when my idea received approval and moved forward to the testing stage, affirming my ability to envision and drive transformative data-driven projects.

2. Scaling Data Cleansing with Python and MySQL

Data cleansing is a foundational step in the journey toward data-driven insights. During my internship, I faced the challenge of processing vast amounts of unstructured data from diverse sources. Leveraging my Python and MySQL expertise, I initiated a comprehensive data scraping and cleansing process, handling over 10,000 web pages and cleansing more than 70,000 data points.
The success of this endeavor lay in my meticulous approach to remove duplicates, standardizing formats, and addressing missing values, ensuring the data was primed for advanced analysis. My dedication to data excellence and attention to detail significantly improved data quality, setting the stage for accurate and reliable insights.

3. Illuminating Insights through Text Mining and TF-IDF Analysis

As a Big Data Engineer Intern, I recognized the untapped potential hidden within raw textual data. I took the initiative to embark on text mining and analyze the data using the CKIP package and TF-IDF technique. By tokenizing, tagging parts of speech, and identifying entities, I gained profound insights into the underlying structure of the textual data.
TF-IDF analysis allowed me to identify the most crucial keywords and terms, providing a clear direction for targeted analysis and revealing valuable patterns and topics that were relevant to H2U's strategic objectives.

4. Building Advanced NLP Machine Learning Models with Neo4j Knowledge Graph Database

Driven by my passion for cutting-edge technologies, I led the development of sophisticated NLP machine-learning models during my internship. Leveraging the power of H2U's Neo4j knowledge graph database, I architected and implemented advanced algorithms to comprehend and extract meaningful insights from interconnected data points.
The NLP models unlocked the full potential of the data repository, empowering H2U to gain deeper customer insights, personalize experiences, and make informed decisions based on data-driven intelligence.

Conclusion

As a Big Data Engineer Intern at H2U, my journey was marked by a relentless pursuit of data excellence, innovation, and transformative impact.

Through my dedication to text mining and TF-IDF analysis, I empowered H2U with valuable insights from raw textual data, steering the company toward informed decision-making and strategic planning.

My time at H2U has been a remarkable experience, and I am grateful for the opportunities to work on impactful projects that have shaped my professional growth. As I continue my journey as a data enthusiast, I carry with me the invaluable lessons and experiences gained during my internship, propelling me forward to create even more transformative data solutions in the future.
